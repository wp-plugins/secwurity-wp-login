SeCWurity WP Login protects your login page against different attack types...

== Description ==

Is there any doubt the security of your site? Do you think that your computer has keylogger virus? Do you think someone has stolen your password? None of this is important when you get this plugin :)
SeCWrity Wp Login is coded for your sites againts brute force attacks, keylogger viruses and more..

== Installation ==

1. Upload folder of plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to Cyber-Warrior > SeCWurity Login and make the settings by yourself.
1. Do not forget to change your password.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= I forgot my password. What should I do? =
